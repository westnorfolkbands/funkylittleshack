About

Born to perform

Five performers from King's Lynn who found each other on the am dram stage — and never really left it. We are Funky Little Shack — and we only play certified bangers.

Weddings, parties, corporate events — if you need a room full of people losing their minds to Motown, Dua Lipa, Shania Twain and Whitney in the same set, we're your band. No fillers. No slow patches. Just two sets of pure floor-fillers delivered with everything we've got.

Also obsessed with Disney? That's what our sister band Big Summer Blowout is for. But tonight? Tonight we go full Funky Little Shack.
