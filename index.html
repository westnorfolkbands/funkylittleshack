About Us

Funky Little Shack — the West Norfolk party starters!

From Motown magic to Dua Lipa bangers (and everything in between), we deliver nothing but floor-fillers to keep your night buzzing. Born from a love of amateur dramatics and a Disney cover project (Big Summer Blowout), we’ve kept the fun alive — and yes, we can still sneak in a Disney hit or two if you ask nicely. Our mission? Simple: keep the party going all night long.

FAQ

Can I book you for our event?

Of course, we'd love to help bring music to your weddings, parties and events,

What do you need?

Firstly a good power supply, ideally at least 2 sockets, even better if there are more. Space wise, we try to work with what you have - the more the better, but as there are 5 of us, PA, lights, a drum kit, amps we need at least 4 x 3 m. We need time to set up - a minimum of 1 hour before you want us to play, and an hour after.
