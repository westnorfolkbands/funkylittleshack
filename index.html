About

Born to perform

We're a party pop band from London making music that's impossible to ignore. We started jamming in a dodgy rehearsal room in 2021 and haven't really stopped since.

Our sound sits somewhere between euphoric hooks, live energy, and the kind of chorus you sing in the shower for three weeks. We've played everything from sticky-floored pub nights to 2,000-cap venues.

New single and EP coming in 2025. Stay close.
